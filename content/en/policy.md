Vinayak Hora ("the app", "we", "us") is a Vedic astrology application. This
policy explains what data we collect, why, where it is stored, and how you can
access or delete it.

## 1. Summary
- Your data is stored **locally on your device** by default.
- If you take a **paid subscription**, you sign in with **Google** and your
  horoscopes are **backed up and synced** to our cloud (Google Firebase) so they
  survive device loss and sync across your devices.
- We **do not sell** your data and **do not use** your email, name, or birth
  details for marketing.

## 2. Information we collect

### a) Account information (Google Sign-In)
When you sign in with Google we receive your **email address, display name, and
profile photo**, and we create an account identifier (uid). This is used to
identify your account, restore your purchases and data across devices, and (for
subscribers) sync your horoscopes.

### b) Horoscope data
Birth details you enter — **name, gender, date and time of birth, place,
latitude/longitude, time zone, altitude** — and any **notes** you add. These are
stored locally. For **subscribers**, this data is also stored in our cloud to
provide backup and cross-device sync. Sample/demo horoscopes are never uploaded.

### c) Subscription & billing data
When you purchase a subscription through Google Play, we store your
**subscription tier, status, renewal/expiry date, product identifier, and the
Google Play purchase token** to provide and validate your entitlement. Payment
itself is processed by **Google Play**; we never receive your card or payment
details.

### d) Device & usage data
- A device **App Set ID** and first-seen timestamp, used to manage the
  introductory ad-free period and prevent abuse.
- **Firebase Analytics** usage and diagnostic data (app events, device model, OS
  version, approximate region) to understand usage and improve the app.
- **Firebase App Check** attestation tokens to protect our backend from abuse.
- For the free tier, **Google AdMob** may use an advertising identifier to show
  ads, subject to the consent choice you make in-app.

### e) App settings
Your preferences (language, theme, dashboard layout, calculation options) are
stored locally on your device.

## 3. Where data is stored
- **On your device:** an encrypted local database and app preferences.
- **In the cloud:** Google **Firebase** (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, App
  Check) and **Google Play** billing. Data may be processed on Google
  infrastructure, including the `asia-south1` (India) region.

## 4. How we use your data
- Provide astrology calculations and the app's features.
- Authenticate you and restore your purchases and data across devices.
- Back up and sync horoscopes for subscribers.
- Validate and manage subscriptions.
- Maintain security and prevent abuse.
- Improve the app through aggregate analytics.

We do **not** use your personal information (email, name, birth details) for
marketing, and we do **not** sell or share it with third parties for their own
marketing.

## 5. Sharing & third-party services
We share data only with service providers that operate the app. These providers
process data under their own privacy policies:
- **Google Play Services / Firebase** (authentication, database, app integrity,
  billing) — https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Firebase Analytics** — https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/
- **Google AdMob** (advertising, free tier only) — https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing.

## 6. Data retention
We retain your cloud data while your account exists. Local data remains on your
device until you delete it or uninstall the app. You can request deletion at any
time (see below).

## 7. Your choices and rights
Depending on your jurisdiction you may have rights to access, correct, or delete
your personal data, and to withdraw consent.

### Deleting your data
- **In the app:** open **Settings** and choose **Delete account & data** (the
  last option). You will be shown the consequences and asked to confirm; on
  confirmation we permanently delete your cloud horoscopes, account, and
  subscription records, and remove your data from this device.
- **Without the app:** email **vinayakhora@gmail.com** from your account email
  address with your deletion request, and we will delete your data within 30 days.

> **Note:** Deleting your data does **not** cancel an active Google Play
> subscription. To stop billing, cancel the subscription in **Google Play →
> Subscriptions**.

## 8. Children
The app is not directed to children under the age required by your local law for
data consent. We do not knowingly collect data from such children.

## 9. Security
Local data is stored in an encrypted database. Cloud data is protected by
per-user access rules so that only your authenticated account can read or write
your records, and by Firebase App Check.

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected
here with an updated "Last updated" date.
