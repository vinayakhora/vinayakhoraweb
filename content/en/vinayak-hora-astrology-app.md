**Vinayak Hora** is a powerful and highly customizable astrology application built for **professional astrologers**, researchers, and serious students of **Vedic (Jyotish)** and **Tropical Astrology**. Designed with precision, depth, and flexibility, Vinayak Hora adapts seamlessly to the unique working style of every astrologer.

The app supports **North Indian and South Indian chart styles** and is available in **Hindi and English**, making it accessible to a wide audience. Charts can be displayed using **traditional planet symbols**, and **font sizes** can be adjusted for maximum readability.

---

## Complete Customization for Astrologers

Vinayak Hora gives astrologers full control over their workspace. Screen layouts are fully customizable — charts and tables can be **added, minimized, rearranged, or removed** according to individual preferences.

Whether you prefer data-rich tables or clean visual charts, Vinayak Hora adapts to your workflow.

The app supports **Divisional Charts from D1 to D60**, all of which can be generated for both:

- **Rāśi (Natal) Charts**
- **Transit (Gochara) Charts**

---

## Advanced Vimshottari Dasha & Strength Analysis

Vinayak Hora offers a deeply customizable **Vimshottari Dasha** system with options to define:

- Number of days in a year  
- Starting (first) Nakshatra  
- Vimshottari Dasha for **any divisional chart**

Advanced strength and evaluation tools include:

- **Shadbal** (Graph & Data Table)
- **Bhava Balas**
- **Ishta / Kashta Phala**
- **Vimshopaka Bala** (6, 7, 10, and 16 Vargas)

---

## Detailed Planetary Analysis

For each planet, Vinayak Hora provides precise and comprehensive astrological details, including:

- Planet degree, Nakshatra, and Pada  
- Jaimini Karaka  
- Planetary dignity  
- Baladi Avastha  
- Jagratadi Avastha  

These details help astrologers perform deeper and more accurate horoscope analysis.

---

## Transits, Prashna & Birth Time Correction

The **Transit (Gochara)** module allows astrologers to traverse time effortlessly — both forward and backward — enabling powerful predictive analysis.

Additional professional tools include:

- **Birth Time Correction Tool**
- **Prashna Kundli**
- **Ashtakavarga**
  - Sarvashtakavarga  
  - Bhinnashtakavarga for all planets  

---

## Notes & Consultation Workflow

Vinayak Hora allows astrologers to record detailed notes for each horoscope using:

- **Typed notes**
- **Voice (oral) dictation**

This makes the app ideal for client consultations, research work, and long-term astrological documentation.

---

## Accurate & Flexible Calculations

Vinayak Hora provides extensive calculation customization options, including:

- True or Apparent planet positions  
- **14 Ayanamsha options**  
  (Lahiri, B. V. Raman, Yukteshwar, Bhasin, and more)
- True or Mean Lunar Nodes  
- Geocentric or Topocentric observer positions  
- Configurable **Chara Karaka** methods  
- Full support for **Tropical Astrology**

All planetary calculations in Vinayak Hora are powered by the **Swiss Ephemeris**, ensuring best-in-class accuracy trusted by astrologers worldwide.
