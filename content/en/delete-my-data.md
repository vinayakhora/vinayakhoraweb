# Account & Data Deletion — Vinayak Hora

**App:** Vinayak Hora (com.vinayakhora.vinayak_hora)
**Developer contact:** vinayakhora@gmail.com

This page explains how to delete your Vinayak Hora account and the data
associated with it.

## Option 1 — Delete from within the app (fastest)
1. Open **Vinayak Hora**.
2. Go to **Settings** and choose **Delete account & data** (the last option).
3. You will be shown what will be deleted and asked to confirm. You may also
   **export your horoscopes to a CSV file** first.
4. On confirmation, your account and data are deleted immediately.

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email
If you cannot use the app, email **vinayakhora@gmail.com** from the Google
account email you used to sign in, with the subject **"Delete my account"**. We
will verify ownership and delete your data within **30 days**.

## What gets deleted
- Your **account** (Google sign-in identity / user record).
- Your **horoscopes and notes** stored in the cloud.
- Your **subscription/entitlement records** stored on our servers.
- Horoscope and app data **on the device** (when you delete from within the app).

## What is retained (and for how long)
- We do **not** retain your personal data after deletion, except where required
  by law (e.g., tax/transaction records related to a purchase), kept only for the
  legally required period and not used for any other purpose.
- Aggregated, **anonymized** analytics that cannot identify you may be retained.

## Important: cancelling a paid subscription
Deleting your data here does **not** cancel an active Google Play subscription.
To stop billing, cancel it separately in **Google Play → Payments &
subscriptions → Subscriptions**.
