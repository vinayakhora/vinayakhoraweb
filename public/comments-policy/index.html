This Comments Policy explains the rules for posting comments on vinayakhora.in (the “Website”), and what happens to the information you provide when you do.

1. Commenting Service

Comments on this Website are powered by Disqus, a third-party commenting service. When you post a comment, Disqus may collect information such as your name, email address, IP address, and any content you submit, in accordance with Disqus’s own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We encourage you to review these before commenting.

We do not control how Disqus stores or processes this data beyond the settings available to us as the site operator.

2. Who Can Comment

Anyone may comment, either as a guest or by signing in through Disqus (including via supported social/email logins). You are responsible for the accuracy of any name or contact detail you provide when commenting.

3. Acceptable Use

We welcome genuine questions, corrections, and discussion related to the post. When commenting, please:

  • Stay on topic and relevant to the post.
  • Be respectful, even in disagreement.
  • Cite sources where you make a factual claim, especially about classical texts or calculations.

4. Prohibited Content

The following are not permitted and may be removed without notice:

  • Spam, advertising, or unsolicited promotional links.
  • Abuse, harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks against the author or other commenters.
  • Impersonation of any person or organization.
  • Sharing of another person’s private or personal information without consent.
  • Content that is unlawful, defamatory, obscene, or infringes on intellectual property rights.
  • Requests for personal horoscope readings, predictions, or paid consultations — these are outside the scope of blog comments; please use the app or vinayakhora@gmail.com instead.

5. Moderation

Comments are not pre-moderated before appearing, but we reserve the right to:

  • Remove or edit any comment, in whole or in part, at our sole discretion.
  • Block or report a commenter who violates this policy, through the tools provided by Disqus.

Removal of a comment does not imply agreement or disagreement with its content — comments reflect the views of their individual authors, not those of Vinayak Hora.

6. No Professional Advice

Comments (whether from the author or other readers) are for general discussion only and do not constitute professional, medical, legal, or financial advice. All astrological content on this Website is provided for entertainment and informational purposes only, consistent with our Terms of Use.

7. Reporting a Comment

If you come across a comment that violates this policy, please report it using Disqus’s built-in flagging feature, or email us directly at vinayakhora@gmail.com with a link to the comment.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Comments Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated “Last Updated” date.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy may be sent to vinayakhora@gmail.com.
